Services

We play music suitable for any event, from weddings to corporate meetings.

corporate events

For your corporate meetings, we provide calm and relaxing music to set the tone for your event, whether you would like jazzy or classical background music.

Weddings

For such an important event, we will be sure that your special day will be complimented by simply amazing music!

Private/Public Concerts

We've saved the best for last! We also have some technically challenging pieces, specifically for the concerts that we perform at. However, if you would like a private showing at a small party or such, feel free to contact us!
